Course Plan

Over four weeks of live workshops and bonus on-demand content, Bharti will help you take control of your journey as a to-be mom.

Kickoff | Personal 1:1 Onboarding with Bharti

I want to get to know each of you personally before we jump into the 4 week course! Hence I will have a one-to-one onboarding call to understand your pregnancy and any specific concerns.

Week 1 | Pregnancy, Nutrition & Fitness [Live]

In the first live workshop, we will understand the key physical and hormonal changes that happen during the 3 trimesters, common pregnancy discomforts and their home remedies, how to have a healthy pregnancy through right nutrition & fitness ( yes we will be moving in this session too! ) and the warning signs to look out for. During the week, I will be sharing material for healthy meal plan, deep breathing exercises and daily stretches videos which you can practice in your own time.

Week 2 | Prenatal Yoga Core Strength [Live]

In the second live workshop, we will be practicing a holistic prenatal yoga routine that will help you to build core strength (focus areas - back, abdomen and pelvic floor) which is vital to have a healthy pain free pregnancy as well as faster recovery. We will end with a relaxing deep breathing & pranayama practice. During the week I will be sharing videos for pelvic floor exercises and positive visualizations which you can practice on your own time.

Week 3 | Birth Preparation, Labor & Delivery [Live]

The third live workshop is all about getting you physically & mentally prepared for labor and birth. We will be talking about Optimal Fetal Positioning, how to avoid tearing, recognizing different stages of labor and when to go the hospital, natural labor pain management techniques for you and your partner, labor positions and medical pain relief options (epidural) that are generally available. During the week I will be sharing labor breathing practice, affirmation audios and pain management cheat cheats

Week 4 | Hip Opening for Birth Preparation [Live]

The final workshop is another prenatal yoga session designed to help you open up the pelvis to help engage the baby and prepare for natural birth. We will end with a relaxing positive visualisation practice to help you reduce anxiety and go in to your delivery with positive attitude ( and that is 90% of the game!) Finally I will be sharing your hospital bag packing checklist, hip opening exercises cheat sheet and a one-to-one call to answer any remaining queries.
